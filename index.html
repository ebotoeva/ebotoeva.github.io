About Me

I am a Lecturer at the School of Computing, University of Kent.

I previously worked at Imperial College London with Alessio Lomuscio and at Free University of Bozen-Bolzano with Diego Calvanese.

Research

My current research interests include:

  • verification of AI systems, including multi-agent closed-loop/reactive systems.
  • integration of neural and symbolic AI
  • explainable AI
  • nested data, MongoDB
  • data access and integration with Knowledge Graphs
  • machine learning for combinatorial optimisation
